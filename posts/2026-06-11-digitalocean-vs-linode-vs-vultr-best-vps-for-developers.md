## Introduction to VPS Hosting
When it comes to hosting a website or application, developers have a wide range of options to choose from. One popular choice is Virtual Private Server (VPS) hosting, which offers a balance between shared hosting and dedicated hosting. In this article, we'll compare three popular VPS providers: DigitalOcean, Linode, and Vultr.
### What is VPS Hosting?
VPS hosting is a type of hosting where a physical server is divided into multiple virtual servers, each with its own operating system and resources. This allows developers to have full control over their server, just like with dedicated hosting, but at a lower cost.
