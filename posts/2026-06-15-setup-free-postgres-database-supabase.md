## Creating a Supabase Account
To get started with Supabase, you need to create an account. Simply visit the Supabase website and sign up using your email address or GitHub account. Once you have verified your email address, you can log in to the Supabase dashboard and start creating your free Postgres database.

### Step 2: Set Up Your Database
Once your project is created, you can set up your Postgres database. Click on the 'Database' tab and select 'Create a new database'. Choose the 'Postgres' database type and enter a name for your database. You can then configure the database settings, including the username, password, and port number.
