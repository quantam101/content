## Understanding VPS Hosting and Its Benefits
Virtual Private Server (VPS) hosting provides a dedicated virtual environment for your website or application, offering more control, flexibility, and scalability compared to shared hosting. With VPS hosting, you can easily upgrade or downgrade resources as needed, making it an ideal choice for small businesses with fluctuating traffic or resource demands. [Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/web-hosting?REFERRALCODE=ALREADYHERE) and [DigitalOcean](https://m.do.co/c/alreadyhere20) are popular VPS hosting providers that offer affordable and reliable plans.
