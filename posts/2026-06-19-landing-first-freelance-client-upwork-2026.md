### Understanding Job Types
Upwork offers a range of job types, including fixed-price, hourly, and recurring jobs. Understand the differences between each type and choose the ones that best fit your skills and schedule. For example, if you're a writer, you may prefer fixed-price jobs, while a web developer may prefer hourly jobs.
