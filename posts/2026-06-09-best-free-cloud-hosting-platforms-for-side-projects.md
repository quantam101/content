## Introduction to Free Cloud Hosting
Free cloud hosting platforms have revolutionized the way we develop, deploy, and manage side projects. With the rise of cloud computing, it's now possible to host your projects online without incurring significant costs. In this article, we'll explore the best free cloud hosting platforms for side projects in 2026, highlighting their features, pricing, and expert reviews.

## What to Look for in a Free Cloud Hosting Platform
When choosing a free cloud hosting platform, there are several factors to consider. These include storage space, bandwidth, scalability, security, and customer support. It's essential to evaluate your project's requirements and choose a platform that meets your needs. For example, if you're building a simple website, you may not need a lot of storage space, but if you're developing a complex application, you'll require more resources.

### Key Features of Free Cloud Hosting Platforms
Some key features to look for in a free cloud hosting platform include:
* Automatic scaling and load balancing
* Built-in security and firewalls
* Easy deployment and management tools
* Integration with popular development frameworks and tools
* Reliable customer support and community resources
